# Cloud Vendor Selection Decision Enabler

A structured, automated Excel-based selection calculator built to accelerate your pre-cloud migration journey. This tool helps organizations evaluate vendors.

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Cloud Vendor Assessment:** Evaluates different cloud vendors (AWS, Azure, GCP, OCI, IBM, Alibaba) on the below parameters
|Parameters|
   |-|
   |**Market Share & Industry Presence**|
   |**Pricing Models & Transparency**|
   |**Compliance & Security**|
   |**Hybrid Cloud Capabilities**|
   |**Enterprise Agreements & Licensing Flexibilities**|
   |**DevOps Integration**|
   |**Global Network Infrastructure & Performance**|
   |**Latency, Throughput & Scalability**|
   |**Failover & High Availability**|
   |**Vendor Lock-in & Portability**| 
   |**Vendor Lock-in & Portability**|
   |**Management & Monitoring Tools**|
   |**Cost Management & Optimization**|
   |**Storage Performance & IOPS**|
   |**Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Analysis**|
   |**Data Transfer & Egress Cost**|
   |**Network Security & Firewalls**|
   |**AI/ML Platform Capabilities**|
   |**Data Analytics & Big Data**|
   |**Customer Support & SLA Levels**|
   |**Developer Ecosystem & Community**|
